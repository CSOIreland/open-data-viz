General Statement

This statement relates to the privacy practices of this Web based application, henceforth referenced as the Application.

The Application respects your personal privacy rights and does not, as a general rule, collect personal information of any kind, without your clear permission.

The Application contains links to other web sites, no responsability is taken for the privacy practices within any of these other web sites. It is recommended to read the privacy statements on other web sites you visit which may collect personally identifiable information.

This privacy statement applies solely to information collected on this Application.

Secure connection

The Application uses an encrypted connection over SSL (Secure Socket Layer) for your privacy and security.

Cookies

This Application uses essential and persistent cookies to support some of its core functions.

You may enable non-essential cookies required by the ShareThis plugin to easily share your findings on a variety of Social Media channels.

You can manage non-essential cookies via the toggle button below or by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. Non-essential cookies are available to improve your experience, however please note that if you disable non-essential cookies then you may not be able to use some features of this Application.

No Cookies are set to personally identify users.

Analytics and Personal Information

The Application does not collect analytical data or personal information.

Your inputs, selections and location (including your GPS coordinates), are not collected by the Application.

The Application offers an optional search-address feature by using the Google Map API(s). For example you can type an address, part of it or a place to get the most relevant geo-location to pin on a map. You are bound by the Google's Terms of Service when using this optional search-address feature; please read also the Google Privacy Policy.

Definitions
Browser

A browser is software that allows a user to access and view web sites / applications (i.e. Chrome, Firefox, Edge).

IP Address

IP Address stands for Internet Protocol Address. Each machine connected to the Internet has a unique address which can be dynamically or statically assigned. The IP address consists of four octects separated by dots (i.e 212.6.125.76).

Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of information, stored in simple text files in your internet browser by a web site or application. Session cookies expire when the browser is closed and persistent cookies have an expiry date.
